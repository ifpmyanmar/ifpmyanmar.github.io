IFP Myanmar 2020 Themes and Activities

Our conference entails over 100 activities from 6 days. Listed down below are the themes of our conference and the overarching descriptions about the activities we will be conducting.

(1)Team Bonding & TrustBuilding Activities

Day 1 – This activity will serve as an icebreaking day to all the delegates and organizers and this day activities will give a foundational understanding of why this conference is being done and why it is needed for our community.

(2) Identity and Diversity Activities(+Empowerment)

Day 2 – This day activities will allow the delegates to dig deeper into themselves as they manage to explore what they are good at and what they need to improve on to become change-makers. These activities will bring out the true selves of all delegates and the people who have been feeling insignificant will finally have a chance to confide in their fellow delegates and ask for consultancy. This will create a better bond among all the delegates, regardless of everything else and at the end of this day, the delegates will be equipped with confidence, self-awareness, self-esteem and empathy for others. Moreover, there will be included empowerment section for each of the 5 days, including leadership and project management- human centre design thinking to foster a sense of self-awareness and effectively empower them to become confident leaders. (for day 2, empowerment section will include leadership empowerment)

(3)Discrimination Activities(+Empowerment)

Day 3 -This day activities will make the delegates to connect the discrimination that is happened to them and the society, share their experiences, creating a safe place in the conference. The activities will also include tackling issues on stereotypes and discrimination, which is one main cause to occur conflicts. (for day 3 empowerment section, project management will be included)

(4)Peace and Conflict Activities

Day 4 - This day activities will include to know the different types of conflict, to consider different sides in communication and how to approach situations and to learn conflict management and connect inner peace(for day 4 empowerment section will include preparing for their mini project for the service day)+cultural night

(5)Community Building Activities (service day)

Day 5 - The delegates will be given a hands-on experience and simulation on how to build a sustainable community on this day. The delegates will go to a primary school on that day and teach the children poems or play games or make them enjoy and happy related to their prepared own projects. The delegates will be allowed to engage in rigorous discussion and presentation activities.

(6)Action Plan and Farewell

Day 6 -- This is the last day of the conference and the delegates will finally engage in an exciting yet most significant part of the conference. The delegates will be split into 6 groups who will all be given a short presentation on how to design a project or a business model after which the delegates will be allowed an hour to start designing their desired project. They will then be allowed 10 minutes each to pitch these projects and after the conference, these ideas will become the actual follow-up projects. So our conference will be spawning at least another 6 follow-up impacts that will then branch out into definitely bigger causes that are worth supporting. IfP Myanmar 2020 conference will then end victoriously and impactfully at 1 pm sharp.
