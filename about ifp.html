Initiative for Peace (IFP) was founded in 2001 by a group of students from UWC (United World College) and staff to promote peace in global, national and regional conflicts. The annual conference follows a year of preparation by our students, who receive training from specialists in the fields of public administration, conflict resolution and negotiation and historical perspectives in order to facilitate an event in the target country for youth leaders from both sides of a conflict.

IfP Myanmar Background

IFP Myanmar is an international conference founded by UWC (United world College) and organized by IFP alumni from various international IFP Conferences including IFP Mae Sot, Thailand, Cambodia, Hanoi and Myanmar as well as a few passionate individuals who had not been affiliated with IFP before. IFP Myanmar was firstly held in Metta Center, Bago, Myanmar in 2018 and now this IFP Myanmar 2020 will be the second time initiative taken by young people from Myanmar willing to execute significant contributions to the regional peace and development through bringing youths together and implementing changes ,one impact at a time. IFP Myanmar 2020 will be co-facilitated by a cohort or 17 organizing committee members. IFP Myanmar 2020 welcomes all diverse individuals form all over Myanmar as well as from those outside of Myanmar to join the Conference happening from May 11th to May 16th in Yangon or outside of Yangon.


IfP Myanmar Vision

IFP Myanmar hopes to become :
➢ A phenomenal organization that not only recognizes the local issues in Myanmar but also tends to them through measurable impacts.
➢ A close-knit community that welcomes every individual willing to seek a way to weave significant changes.
➢ An efficient intermediary between young people and Myanmar that make ourselves feel more connected so as to foster a unified force that can manage tremendous success in peace-making.


IfP Myanmar Mission

IFP Myanmar 2020 anticipates to recruit young people from all backgrounds-
➢ To let them come together and contribute as a big team to the national welfare and peaceful environment through interactive activities, simulation and presentation sessions as well as team bonding sessions.
➢ To let them become adequately supplied with leadership and management capacities to launch their very own community development and peace initiative projects that will sustain and succeed.
➢ To give them an access to an environment where they will feel welcomed , inspired, empowered and undifferentiated.
